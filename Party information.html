Political Parties in India

Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)

The Bharatiya Janata Party is one of the major political parties in India. It is a right-wing party and is currently the ruling party at the national level.

Website: https://www.bjp.org/

Indian National Congress (INC)

The Indian National Congress is one of the oldest political parties in India. It is a centrist party and has a significant presence across the country.

Website: https://www.inc.in/

Aam Aadmi Party (AAP)

The Aam Aadmi Party is a relatively new political party that emerged out of the anti-corruption movement in India. It is known for its focus on issues such as governance and transparency.

Website: https://www.aamaadmiparty.org/

Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP)

Bahujan Samaj Party is a national political party in India that represents the Bahujans, referring to Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, and Other Backward Classes, along with religious minorities.

Website: http://www.bspindia.org/
